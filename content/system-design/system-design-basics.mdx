System design is the practice of choosing components and trade-offs that meet a product’s needs.

## Begin with requirements

Clarify users, expected traffic, latency, availability, and consistency needs before drawing boxes.

## Design for the bottleneck

Caching, queues, replicas, and partitions are useful only when they address a real workload constraint.
