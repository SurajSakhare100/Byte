Both frameworks let teams build mobile applications for more than one platform from a shared codebase.

## Choose for your team

React Native is a natural fit for JavaScript and React teams. Flutter is compelling when a team prefers Dart and highly controlled rendering.

## Evaluate the product

Prototype a critical screen, test device APIs, and consider the existing skills of the people who will maintain the app.
