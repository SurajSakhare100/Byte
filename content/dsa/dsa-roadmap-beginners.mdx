Interview preparation improves when you learn patterns instead of collecting isolated solutions.

## Build foundations first

Practice arrays, strings, hash maps, stacks, queues, trees, and graphs with simple implementations.

## Review the pattern

After every problem, explain the approach, complexity, and the signal that tells you to use that pattern.
